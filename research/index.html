    One Century of Innovation in Europe and the US
    Bergeaud, Antonin, and Verluise, Cyril
    2021
    Abs

    Innovation is an important driver of potential growth. Long term dynamics of innovation inthe major R&D countries are hardly documented due to the lack of data. In this paper weintroduce a novel dataset on the location and nature of patentees from the 19thcentury inGermany, France, Great Britain and the United States of America. Building on our noveldataset, we document key facts on the direction and magnitude of innovation flows, thegeography of innovation, the role of immigration on innovation and the nature of inventorsover a century, both within and across countries. In all these dimensions, we find a largedegree of heterogeneity

  2. Work in Progress
    The International Diffusion of Breakthrough Technologies
    Verluise, Cyril, and Bergeaud, Antonin
    2021
    Abs

    Building on the nascent automated patent landscaping literature, we introduce a newapproach to study the international diffusion of technologies. We deploy and validate ourapproach on the genome editing technology. We document a significant technological lagbetween leading and laggard countries. This lag is even larger when we consider the country of origin of inventors and assignees instead of the granting offices.
